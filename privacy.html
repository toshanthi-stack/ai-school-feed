AI School - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-14

AI School ("the app") is published by Lilly Tech Systems LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Summary

AI School does not require an account, and the app itself does not collect personal information about you.

Information we collect

  • No personal data collected by the app. The app has no login, no analytics SDK, and no advertising SDK. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or device identifiers.
  • Content delivery. The app downloads its lesson catalog and streams audio over encrypted HTTPS from our content host. As with any website, the hosting provider may record standard server logs (such as IP address and request time). These are not used to build a profile of you.
  • In-app browser. Some lessons let you open the full written lesson on our website (lillytechsystems.com) inside an in-app browser. Pages you open there are subject to the website's own privacy practices, which may include cookies or analytics operated by the website. The app does not read or store that information.

Information we share

We do not sell or share personal information. The app contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Children

AI School is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data security

All network communication uses encrypted HTTPS connections.

Changes to this policy

If the app later adds features that collect data (for example, advertising or accounts), we will update this policy and the app's store data-safety disclosure before those features ship.
